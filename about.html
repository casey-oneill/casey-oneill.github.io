About

An overview of the technologies that I have experience working with, and some of my personal and professional interests.

Personal
I am a fourth-year Computer Science and Arts student majoring in philosophy at the University of New Brunswick. Computer Science and philosophy may seem like unusual pairing, but I believe they complement each other well. Both subjects encourage critical thinking and abstract thought, and the ethics of Computer Science is an interesting and largely unexplored topic.

I chose to pursue a degree in Computer Science because I love to build things - and building things using computers has never been more accessible! I am currently interested in developing interactive applications, such as video games and responsive front-end applications.
