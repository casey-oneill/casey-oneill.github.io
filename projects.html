Self-Directed Research Project
Studying Timer-Based Mechanics in Video Games

Under the supervision of Dr. Daniel Rea, Co-Director of the UNB HCI Lab, I am working to complete a self-directed study on the impact of timer-based mechanics in video games. I am currently working to create a implementations of various Choose Your Own Adventure stories using the Twine game engine. Timer-based mechanics will be added semi-randomly, and participants of the study will respond to surveys regarding their experience of this game.
